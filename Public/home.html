About Us

BiildSoft Publications is dedicated to advancing scholarly communication by providing a platform for high-quality, peer-reviewed research across various academic fields. Their commitment to open access ensures that research is freely accessible to everyone, fostering collaboration and innovation in the academic community. By unifying diverse disciplines, BiildSoft aims to support interdisciplinary research and facilitate the dissemination of knowledge globally. This model not only enhances visibility for authors but also allows readers to engage with a broad spectrum of scientific findings.

The journal is focused on advancing medical and related fields such as dentistry, clinical research, nursing, physiotherapy, occupational therapy, pharmaceutical research, biopharmaceutics, pharmacokinetics, pharmaceutical medicinal chemistry, computational chemistry, pharmacy practice, clinical & hospital pharmacy, pharmacology, genomics and proteomics, and pharmacogenomics.

BiildSoft Publishers' commitment to quick and reliable open-access publishing is a great approach to enhance the dissemination of research. By ensuring that all published works meet rigorous technical and ethical standards, they help maintain the integrity of scholarly communication. Providing a permanent DOI through CrossRef also enhances the visibility and accessibility of research, making it easier for others to cite and share these works. This model can significantly benefit both researchers and the broader community by fostering collaboration and advancing knowledge across various fields.
